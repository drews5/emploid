How it actually works

One search, a clearer signal, and a direct path to the employer.

01

We scan everything

One search covers every major job board and thousands of company career pages. LinkedIn, Indeed, Handshake, Glassdoor, and more.

02

We score for trust

Our Listing Trust Score analyzes posting age, repost frequency, salary transparency, and hiring activity to estimate if a job is real. Scores range from 0 (likely fake) to 100 (likely real).

03

You apply directly

Every apply link routes straight to the employer's own website. No middlemen, no redirects, no third-party portals.

We built this because job searching shouldn't feel like a part-time job.

To anyone burning hours on job boards: the modern job hunt is broken. Companies leave filled positions online to look like they're growing, or to collect resumes for the future. You end up spending hours crafting cover letters for roles that were never truly open.

We built Emploid to fix this. By tracking how long a job has been posted, whether the link goes directly to the employer, and the company's actual hiring patterns, we created a trust filter that works.

Our origin

Emploid started as a class project for IBUS 3055 at the Carlson School of Management. We were five students frustrated with the same problem every job seeker faces: wasting time on listings that lead nowhere.

We use heuristic analysis of posting age, repost frequency, recent hiring activity, and public financial data to estimate the likelihood that a job is real. And we get you directly to hiring managers — not middlemen.

No ads. No dark patterns.

We don't accept payments to boost listings. Our search algorithm prioritizes role relevance, salary transparency, and Listing Trust Score — in that order. If a listing looks fake, we'll tell you. If a company hasn't actually hired anyone in months, you'll see that too.
